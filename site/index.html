Watch together, perfectly in sync

A browser extension that locks YouTube and Crunchyroll playback across friends. Press play, pause or seek on the real player — everyone follows along. No streaming, no accounts, no tracking.

Get the extension View on GitHub

Works on Chrome & Firefox · YouTube & Crunchyroll

Built to feel locked

Sync quality is the whole point — not a "press play now" hack.

Real-player sync

Play, pause and seek on the actual YouTube or Crunchyroll player. Your action is sent to the room and everyone follows — no extra buttons to learn.

Core

Drift correction

A shared room clock and scheduled commands keep everyone aligned. Small gaps are nudged with playback rate; big gaps seek. Paused states converge exactly.

Core

Auto-open the video

Friends just enter the room code. The extension opens the host's exact video for them — no hunting for the right link.

Available

Friend mode

Anyone can play and pause; the host keeps the big controls like large seeks and media changes. Casual by default, no babysitting.

Available

Private by default

It only sends playback state — play, pause, position, rate. No media, no cookies, no logins, no tracking. Rooms are temporary and nothing is stored.

Always

Chrome & Firefox

The same extension runs on Chromium browsers and Firefox. Reconnects automatically if your browser drops the connection.

Available

Three steps

1

Create a room

Open a video, click the extension, and create a room. You get a short code to share.

2

Friends join

They paste the code. The extension opens your video for them automatically.

3

Watch in sync

Play, pause and seek on the player. Everyone stays locked to the same moment.

In sync

Room clock, scheduled commands and drift correction keep playback tight.

Private

Sync playback, not content. No media, credentials or DRM data ever leave your browser.

No account

No sign-up, no telemetry. Rooms are ephemeral and disappear when everyone leaves.

Get Watch Party Sync

Free and open source. Install manually from the latest GitHub release.

Chrome / Edge / Brave

Download the -chrome.zip, unzip it, open chrome://extensions, enable Developer mode and click Load unpacked.

Download for Chrome

Firefox

Download the -firefox.zip, unzip it, open about:debugging and use Load Temporary Add-on to pick the manifest.
